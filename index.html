Settings & sync
Sync via Microsoft OneDrive
OneDrive sync

Sign in with your Microsoft account to sync your data across all devices via OneDrive. Do this once per device.

Each button signs you in fresh with Microsoft, then immediately pulls or pushes your data. No session is saved.

How it works

Click Sign in & pull to load your latest data from OneDrive, or Sign in & push to save your current data to OneDrive. Each button redirects you to Microsoft to sign in fresh — no password is remembered. Works identically on every device and browser.

Data management

Export saves a backup file to your device. Clear removes local browser data only — your Gist copy is unaffected.
